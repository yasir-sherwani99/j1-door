Common Garage Door Problems

A common reason for your garage door not working is a broken spring and you can usually diagnose this fairly easily. Springs are typically directly above the garage door near the wall that provides tension to help your garage door opener lift the garage door. It’s important to note that if you have two springs on your garage door, you’ll most likely need to have both springs replaced.

Note: Please do not attempt to repair or replace a spring on your own, call a professional garage door technician as this repair can be hazardous.
