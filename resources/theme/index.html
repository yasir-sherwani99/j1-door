Why Choose J1 Door?

J1 Door Company™ is a premier provider of door and access solutions, serving residential, commercial, institutional, and industrial sectors. Since its inception, the company has been a trailblazer in the industry, inventing the first upward-acting door in 1921, the first electric garage door opener in 1926, and the first automatic sliding door in 1954.
